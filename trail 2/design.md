# Design System: Aatithya360 Futuristic AI Landing Page
**Project ID:** 10724121254528473399

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The design embodies a **High-Tech, Futuristic, and Neural** aesthetic. It uses a **Dark Mode** canvas to create a sense of depth and mystery, punctuated by vibrant glowing elements that represent "intelligence" and "connectivity." The atmosphere is dense yet structured, conveying the complexity of AI-driven hospitality and manpower solutions through a clean, scientific lens.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
The system is built on a deep, dark foundation with high-contrast electric accents.

*   **Midnight Base (#000000 to #0F172A):** The primary background color, providing a sleek, professional stage for holographic elements.
*   **Electric Digital Blue (#136DEC):** The primary action and signature brand color. Used for buttons, glowing icons, and focused neural paths.
*   **Neural Cyan (#22D3EE):** Secondary accent color for data visualizations, borders, and subtle "energy" gradients.
*   **Text Primary (#FFFFFF):** High-contrast white for headers and key body text to ensure absolute legibility in dark mode.
*   **Faded Interface Gray (#94A3B8):** Used for non-essential labels and inactive states, maintaining a clean visual hierarchy.

## 3. Typography Rules
*   **Font Family:** `Space Grotesk` (Sans-Serif) - A geometric, monospaced-influenced font that perfectly captures the "Futuristic AI" vibe.
*   **Headers:** Heavy weights (Bold/Semibold) with tight letter-spacing to feel impactful and authoritative.
*   **Body:** Medium/Regular weights with slightly increased line-height (`1.6`) for comfortable reading amidst complex layouts.

## 4. Component Stylings
*   **Buttons:** 
    *   **Shape:** Subtly rounded corners (`rounded-lg` / 8px).
    *   **Styling:** Solid `Electric Blue` fills with white text, or transparent outlines with blue glowing borders.
    *   **Effect:** Subtle outer-glow on hover to simulate "powered-on" states.
*   **Cards/Containers:** 
    *   **Surface:** Semi-transparent dark surfaces with a glassmorphism backdrop blur (`backdrop-blur-md`).
    *   **Borders:** Ultra-thin, 1px borders using `Neural Cyan` with low opacity (`border-cyan-500/20`).
    *   **Roundness:** Consistent 8px rounding (`rounded-lg`).
*   **Inputs/Forms:** 
    *   **Background:** Deep recessed gray-blacks.
    *   **Border:** Glows `Electric Blue` when focused, creating a high-tech "activation" feel.

## 5. Layout Principles
*   **Neural Grid:** Alignment follows a modular grid with generous whitespace to prevent the dense information from feeling cluttered.
*   **Elevation:** Layers are created using backdrop blurs and subtle neon rim-lights rather than traditional shadows.
*   **Global Elements:** Use of "Heatmaps," "Neural Processing Interfaces," and "Holographic Overlays" to reinforce the AI-first identity.
